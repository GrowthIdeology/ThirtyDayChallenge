## Our Core Principle

We collect nothing. Your data stays on your device.

---

## 1. Overview

Growth Ideology ("we", "us", "our") operates the **30-Day Challenge** iOS application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding your information when you use the App.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

---

## 2. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of the following:

- Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number)
- Location data
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs (IDFA)
- Usage analytics or crash reports sent to our servers
- In-app behavior or interaction data
- Health or financial data entered into challenges
- Photos, contacts, calendar data, or any other device data
- IP addresses or network information

We have no servers, no databases, and no backend. There is nothing to breach.

---

## 3. Data Stored on Your Device

The App stores the following data **exclusively on your device's local storage** using Apple's standard file system and UserDefaults:

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Challenge progress (checked items, daily scores, notes, reflections) | To track your progress |
| App preferences (selected theme, active challenge) | To remember your settings |
| Purchase records for unlocked premium challenges | To restore purchases without re-buying |

This data:
- **Never leaves your device**
- Is not backed up to our servers (it may be included in your personal iCloud backup if you have iCloud Backup enabled in iOS Settings — this is controlled entirely by you and Apple)
- Is not associated with any account or identity

---

## 4. In-App Purchases

Premium challenges are sold as **one-time, non-consumable purchases** through Apple's App Store.

- All payment processing is handled entirely by **Apple Inc.**
- We receive only a confirmation that a purchase was completed — no payment details, card information, or personal data is shared with us
- Purchase records are stored locally on your device via Apple's StoreKit framework
- You can restore your purchases at any time using the "Restore Purchases" button in Settings

Apple's handling of payment data is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

---

## 5. Internet Access

The App requires **no internet connection** to function.

Internet access is used **only** in these specific cases:

1. At the moment of an in-app purchase — to contact Apple's servers to process the transaction
2. When you tap external links (e.g., our Instagram page) — which opens your browser

No other network requests are made by this App at any time.

---

## 6. Third-Party Services & SDKs

The App uses **only one** third-party framework:

| Framework | Provider | Purpose |
|-----------|----------|---------|
| StoreKit | Apple Inc. | Processing in-app purchases |

We do **not** use:
- Analytics SDKs (Firebase, Mixpanel, Amplitude, etc.)
- Advertising networks (AdMob, Facebook Audience Network, etc.)
- Crash reporting tools (Crashlytics, Sentry, etc.)
- Social login SDKs
- Any other third-party code that phones home

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

This App does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

The App contains no features that require or enable the submission of personal data. There are no accounts, no login, and no network communication with our servers.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has somehow provided personal information to us, please contact us at [lamhotsiagian2025@gmail.com](mailto:lamhotsiagian2025@gmail.com) and we will address your concern promptly.

## 9. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:

- **Reset challenge progress:** Go to Settings → Reset Progress
- **Delete all app data:** Uninstall the App from your device. This permanently removes all locally stored data.

We have no data to delete on our end because we never collected any.

---

## 10. Security

Because we collect no data and operate no servers, there is no risk of a server-side data breach affecting your personal information. Your data is protected by your device's own security (Face ID, Touch ID, passcode encryption).
