## Frequently Asked Questions

### Getting Started

**How do I start a challenge?**  
Open the app and tap the **Library** tab (bottom navigation). Tap any challenge to set it as your active challenge. Your progress begins on Day 1 automatically.

**Can I do more than one challenge at a time?**  
The app tracks one active challenge at a time. You can switch between installed challenges in the Library tab — your progress on each is saved separately.

**What does a typical day look like?**  
Each day has three sessions: **Morning**, **Afternoon**, and **Evening** — each with 3 personalized checklist items. There is also a daily challenge task, a reflection section, a score tracker, and a notes area.

### Progress & Data

**Where is my data stored?**  
All data is stored **locally on your device only**. No account, no internet, no cloud. Your progress, notes, scores, and reflections never leave your phone.

**Will I lose my progress if I reinstall the app?**  
Yes — because data is stored locally with no cloud backup, uninstalling the app will erase your progress. To preserve your data, make sure **iCloud Backup** is enabled on your device (Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → iCloud Backup). Your app data will be included in your device backup.

**How do I reset my progress?**  
Go to **Settings** (gear icon, bottom right) → scroll to your active challenge → tap **Reset Progress (Start Over)**. This permanently erases checked items, scores, and notes for that challenge.

### Purchases

**What are Premium Challenges?**  
Premium Challenges are additional 30-day programs available as a one-time in-app purchase. Once purchased, they are yours to keep forever — no subscriptions, no renewals.

**How do I restore a purchase on a new device?**  
Go to **Settings** → **Purchases** → tap **Restore Purchases**. Make sure you are signed into the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

**I purchased a challenge but it shows as locked. What do I do?**  
Try tapping **Restore Purchases** in Settings. If the issue persists, contact us at [lamhotsiagian2025@gmail.com](mailto:lamhotsiagian2025@gmail.com) with your Apple ID purchase receipt and we'll help resolve it.

**Can I get a refund?**  
Refunds are handled by Apple. You can request a refund at [reportaproblem.apple.com](https://reportaproblem.apple.com). We do not process refunds directly.

**The in-app purchase price shows a different currency. Why?**  
The price is automatically shown in your local currency by Apple based on your App Store region. The price displayed in the app is pulled live from Apple's servers.

### Technical Issues

**The app crashed. What should I do?**  
1. Force-quit the app and reopen it
2. Make sure your iOS is up to date (Settings → General → Software Update)
3. Restart your device
4. If the crash persists, email us at [lamhotsiagian2025@gmail.com](mailto:lamhotsiagian2025@gmail.com) with your device model and iOS version

**The share card doesn't appear after tapping Share. What's wrong?**  
The app needs permission to access your Photo Library to save images. Go to **Settings → Privacy & Security → Photos → 30-Day Challenge** and select **Add Photos Only** or **Full Access**.

**A checklist item isn't saving. What do I do?**  
Make sure you are tapping the checkbox directly (not the text). The checkbox should animate with a green checkmark when tapped. If it still doesn't save, force-quit and reopen the app.

**The app is in English but I want Indonesian (or vice versa). How do I switch?**  
Each challenge has a language version. In the **Library** tab, look for the challenge with "EN" for English or "ID" for Indonesian. They are separate challenges — you can switch between them freely.
